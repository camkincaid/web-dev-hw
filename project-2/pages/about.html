About Me

Who am I...

I am a first time website coder and love cooking and traveling. I am currently a senior studying business marketing and I am on the UM tennis team. In my free time I enjoy cooking or watching shows such as Bizzare Foods, Food Network Star and Chopped! Last summer I traveled to Europe for the first time visitng countries such as Italy, Switzerland, Austria, Monaco and France. Obviously the food I ate there was incredible!

The Project

For this project, I chose an instructable on "Spaghetti all Carbonara" a classic Italian dish. The reason I chose this dish was because I swear the carbonara dish I tried in Rome was one of the best plates of food I'd had in my life. No joke. The Italian restaurants in America such as Olive Garden and Macaroni Grill in no way produce the same pasta texture and flavor as the pasta I tasted in Italy. So, I wanted to find a homemade recipe to duplicate my wonderful experience.

The Process

I learned so much during my process of creating this website. I had never used HTML before so it was exciting to learn new things, experiment with trial and error and in the end see a super cool result that I created myself!
