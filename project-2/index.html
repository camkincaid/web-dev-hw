Traditional Italian Carbonara Recipe

Over the past summer, while traveling in Europe, I experienced the best carbonara dish of my life in Rome, Italy. The flavor of the pecorino cheese combined with perfectly cooked homemade pasta and pieces of melting pancetta were literally mouth blowing. So, coming back to America, I was determined to find a recipe online which could resemble the classic Italian dish. This recipe is originally from allrecipes.

>
There's nothing more romantic than Italian food -Elisha Cuthbert

Ingredients

  • 2tsp olive oil
  • 1 pound guinciale (cured pork cheek), diced
  • 1 (16 ounce) package spaghetti
  • 3 eggs
  • 10Tbs grated percorino Romano cheese, divided
  • salt to taste
  • ground black pepper to taste

Special Notes

Pecorino Romano
Italian cheese made from 100% sheeps milk, a sharper, salty alternative to parmesan
Guinciale
An italian cured meat product prepared from pork jowl or cheek. An alternative to guinciale is pancetta, an Italian bacon made of pork belly meat
Italian Pasta
It is best to use the Italian brand "Barilla" which can be found at your local grocery stores for just $2.99 ...now only $1.39
